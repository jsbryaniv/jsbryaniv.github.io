# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and shared when you visit `https://jsbryaniv.com` (the “Site”).

## 1. Information Collected

### a) Information you provide directly

If you contact me through the contact form, you may provide personal information such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Message content

### b) Information collected automatically

When you use the Site, certain technical information may be collected automatically, including:

- IP address (or a shortened/anonymized version where applicable)
- Browser type and version
- Device type
- Pages visited
- Referring pages
- Time and date of visits
- Interaction data (for example, page views and session activity)

## 2. Analytics and Tracking

This Site uses **Google Analytics** to understand website usage and improve content.

Google Analytics may set cookies or use similar technologies to collect usage information. Data is processed according to Google’s policies.

- Learn more: `https://policies.google.com/privacy`
- Google Analytics opt-out: `https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout`

## 3. Contact Form Processing

The Site uses **Formspree** to process contact form submissions. Information you submit through the form is sent to Formspree for delivery and handling.

Formspree’s privacy practices are available at:
`https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/`

## 4. How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

- Respond to contact requests
- Operate and maintain the Site
- Analyze traffic and improve content, usability, and performance
- Detect misuse, spam, or security issues

## 5. Sharing of Information

I do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared with trusted service providers only as needed to operate the Site, including:

- Google (analytics)
- Formspree (contact form processing)
- Hosting/CDN providers (infrastructure delivery)

Information may also be disclosed if required by law or to protect legal rights.

## 6. Data Retention

Contact form submissions and analytics data may be retained for as long as needed for operational, communication, security, and legal purposes.

## 7. Your Choices

You may:

- Disable cookies in your browser settings
- Use the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on
- Avoid submitting personal information through the contact form

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights regarding access, correction, deletion, or objection to processing of personal information.

## 8. Third-Party Links

This Site may include links to third-party websites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites.

## 9. Data Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect information, but no method of internet transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 10. Children’s Privacy

This Site is not intended for children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest revision.

## 12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact me through the [Contact](/contact/) page.
